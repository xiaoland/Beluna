# SOP: Solidify

## Role

Use when findings must be restated into stable claims, contracts, or explicit decisions.

## Forbidden

- Do not let mode selection hide the durable owner of the truth.
- Do not update the wrong layer just because it is convenient.

## Read-Do Steps

1. Restate the target, owner, invariants, scope boundaries, and likely affected files.
2. Confirm which claims, contracts, or operational truths are becoming durable.
3. For Beluna, await human confirmation before updating durable docs.
4. Update only the authoritative owner layer, then record downstream implications if they exist.

## Exit Criteria

- Durable ownership is explicit.
- The stable claim or contract is recorded in the correct layer.
- Downstream technical implications are clear.
