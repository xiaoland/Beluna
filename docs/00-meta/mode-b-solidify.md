# SOP: Solidify

## Role

Use when findings must be restated into stable claims, contracts, decisions, or promotion candidates.

This mode often bridges tasks and durable docs. For non-trivial code work, use implementation taste to form the temporary design claim and prepare the verification shape.

## Forbidden

- Do not let mode selection hide the durable owner of the truth.
- Do not update the wrong layer just because it is convenient.
- Do not promote unstable guesses into PRD, TDD, deployment docs, or local AGENTS.

## Read-Do Steps

1. Gather the current findings, evidence, and assumptions.
2. Decide which truths are stable enough to promote and which must stay in tasks.
3. Restate the target, owner, invariants, scope boundaries, likely affected files, and verification.
4. Confirm which claims, contracts, or operational truths are becoming durable.
5. For Beluna, await human confirmation before updating durable docs.
6. Update only the authoritative owner layer, then record downstream implications if they exist.

## Exit Criteria

- Durable ownership is explicit.
- Verification is explicit.
- The stable claim or contract is recorded in the correct layer, or the next edit is safe to perform.
