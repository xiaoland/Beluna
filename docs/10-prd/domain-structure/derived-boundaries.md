# Derived Boundaries

Boundary derivation rule:

1. Start from accepted product drivers and behavior claims.
2. Partition stable business meaning where vocabulary, lifecycle, and local rules repeatedly cohere.
3. Stabilize only after repeated use confirms the boundary reduces ambiguity.

## Current Derived Domains

1. Cognition Continuity
- Focus: continuity of intent, memory, and post-recovery behavioral coherence.
- Derived from: Claim 1 and incident-handling workflow.

2. World Interaction
- Focus: endpoint-mediated interaction and explicit action outcomes.
- Derived from: Claim 2 and endpoint integrator workflow.

3. Operational Trust
- Focus: observability, bounded recovery, and reliable operational control.
- Derived from: Claim 3 and operator runtime workflow.

## Constraint

Domain boundaries documented here cannot introduce new upstream product obligations not already present in `_drivers` or `behavior`.
