# Cross-Domain Interactions

## Interaction Patterns

1. Cognition Continuity <-> World Interaction
- Product interaction outcomes feed continuity quality.
- Continuity quality influences subsequent interaction relevance.

2. World Interaction <-> Operational Trust
- Endpoint failures surface as operational signals.
- Operational controls constrain and protect interaction reliability.

3. Operational Trust <-> Cognition Continuity
- Recovery behavior affects continuity validity.
- Continuity-state reliability affects post-incident service confidence.

## Cross-Domain Rule

Cross-domain interactions must be explained in product terms first. Mechanism realization belongs in `20/30/40`.
