# Capabilities

Beluna capability statements are product-facing and implementation-agnostic.

## Core Capabilities

1. Maintain continuity of agent cognition over ongoing operation.
2. Accept natural-language interaction at product boundaries.
3. Dispatch world-facing actions through endpoint integrations with explicit terminal outcomes.
4. Recover service operation under bounded failure and shutdown scenarios.
5. Provide operator-grade runtime visibility for diagnosis and control.

## Capability Boundaries

1. Capabilities here define required product behavior, not module decomposition.
2. Mechanism choices are governed by Product TDD, Unit TDD, and deployment docs.
