# Business And Service Objectives

## Objectives

1. Deliver a survival-oriented digital life runtime that is operable by a small builder/operator team.
2. Keep service behavior legible so incidents can be diagnosed and recovered without rediscovering architecture from code.
3. Preserve extension velocity through pluggable endpoints while keeping product behavior coherent.
4. Control reliability and operating-cost risk with explicit contracts and guardrails.

## Service-Level Success Shape

Success means Beluna can be operated continuously, extended safely, and evaluated against explicit product claims instead of implicit implementation assumptions.
